Class ResourceFacingServiceAtomic

This is an abstract base class that defines a stand-alone ResourceFacingService that meets the needs of a particular CustomerFacingService. Stand-alone ResourceFacingServices may be linked directly to a CustomerFacingService or aggregated by a ResourceFacingServiceComposite.
