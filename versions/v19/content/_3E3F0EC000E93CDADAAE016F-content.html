Class PhysicalPort

This class represents an actual or potential end point of a topological (physical) link, and corresponds directly to a physical port on a topology map. PhysicalPorts are always contained by another physical object - they can't exist by themselves. The two most common examples are PhysicalPorts on a Card and on a Chassis.
