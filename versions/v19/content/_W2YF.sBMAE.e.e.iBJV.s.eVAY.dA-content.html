Diagram Figure RS.05 - HostingPlatformRequirement and Capacity
The ResourceCapacityDemand represents the amount of ResourceCapacity that is required, planned to be consumed or has been consumed.

The HostingPlatformRequirementsSpec is used to represent the common characteristics and associations of resources in the infrastructure (e.g. compute, storage, network) for a SoftwareSpecification.
The HostingPlatformRequirementsSpec requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum...) of capacity required.

A HostingPlatformRequirement implements a HostingPlatformRequirementSpec for a specific InstalledSoftware. It requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum...) of capacity planned or consumed.
Refer to Capacity guide book for more explanation about the Capacity model.
