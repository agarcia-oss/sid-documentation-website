Class LoyaltyAction

A LoyaltyAction may correspond either to a CustomerOrder (Ex: 100 SMS free), or to an Interaction (Ex: a SMS notifying the 100 SMS free) or to a LoyaltyEarn (Ex: 100 points on the LoyaltyAccount)
