Business Partner Domain::Applied Party Billing Rate ABE
Class AppliedPartyPenaltyCharge

The AppliedPartyPenaltyCharge is a type of AppliedPartyBillingCharge.
It represents penalty charges such as late fees, payment rejection fees, interests, that could be applied, linked to dunning of a due amount, in order to bill the party according to contractual terms and conditions.
