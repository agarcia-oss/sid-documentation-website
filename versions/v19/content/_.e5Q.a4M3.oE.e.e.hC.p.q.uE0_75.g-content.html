Business Partner Domain::Party Account ABE
Class PartyAccountAssignment

The PartyAccountAssignment specifies rules for a PartyAccount on how assignment to a FinancialAccount has to be done for each accounting event.

Attributes
Boolean  isDefault

If True means that the PartyAccount's accounting events are logged by default into the corresponding FinancialAccount.
String  assignmentPurpose

This attribute indicates why the PartyAccount is related to the FinancialAccount. For example, for taxes, revenue associated with an invoice, bill, or credit memo, payment, and so forth.
