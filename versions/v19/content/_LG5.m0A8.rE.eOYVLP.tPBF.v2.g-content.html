Metric Definition Measure ABE

The MetricDefMeasure specifies a measure of a specific aspect of an entity and is the main part of a MetricDefinition.
The Metric Definition Measure ABE contains all entities describing the MetricDefMeasure such as the related threshold, notification and consequence.
