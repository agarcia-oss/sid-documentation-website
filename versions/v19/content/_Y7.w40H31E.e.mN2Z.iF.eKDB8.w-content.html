Diagram Figure Pr.32b - Product Price Alteration hierarchy
The following diagram shows the ProdPriceAlteration hierarchy.
• A ReplacementProdPriceAlteration represents a new price that replaces a charge for a Product.
• A DiscountProdPriceAlteration represents a discount (a reduction of price) for a Product.
• A TaxProdPriceAlteration is a type of ProdPriceAlteration that applies a TaxDefinition.
