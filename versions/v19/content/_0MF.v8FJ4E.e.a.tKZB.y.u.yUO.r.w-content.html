Class PartyRevShareReconDiscrepancy

A difference between information received from another party with the revenue share determined by an enterprise that is to be resolved. For example, an enterprise's calculated revenue share for a product offering may be missing a product that has been acquired that appears on an bill/invoice from another party.

Note: This entity is related to two of any combination of PartyPayment, PartyPaymentItem, ComponentShareProdOfferItem, AppliedPartyBillingRate.
