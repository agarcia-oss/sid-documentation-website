Diagram Figure PrPs.02 - Party Role Prod Spec Subclasses
The Figure PrPs.02 - Party Role Prod Spec Subclasses depicts the different types of PartyRoleProductSpecification that could be used.
A ProductSpecification may sold / rented by the Service Provider (SalesProductSpecification) or bought by the Service Provider (PruchaseProductSpecification).
For a SalesProductSpecification the Service Provider might:
• Either only take the order on behalf of another Party (OrderedOnlySalesProdSpec),
• Or take the order and fulfill the product (FulfilledSalesProdSpec).
