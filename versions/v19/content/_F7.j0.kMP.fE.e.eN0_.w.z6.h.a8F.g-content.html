Product Domain::Product ABE::Usage Volume Product ABE
Class UsageVolumeProduct

A UsageVolumeProduct is a type of ProductComponent described by a UsageVolumeProductSpec that carries UsageVolumeBalances.
A UsageVolumeProduct relies on one or many NetworkProducts.
