One or many PartyRoles might be involved in a BusinessPartnerProductOrder / BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem such as Distributor, Holder, Buyer or Product User.
Each BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem requires an action (AllowedProductAction) on a ProductSpecification or a ProductOffering.
The configuration required is described by the related Product even if it isn’t delivered yet. The prices applicable are related to the Product (ProductPrice).
To deliver the corresponding Product, the Place to deliver and Appointment(s) might be needed. An Appointment is an arrangement to do something or meet someone at a particular time and location.
A BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem may also specify the commitment chosen by the Service Provider (i.e. CommitmentTermOrCondition).
The BusinessPartnerProductOrderItems may specify the BusinessPartnerAccount to which the Products’ are charged to.
When BusinessPartnerProductOrderItems are not charged to a BusinessPartnerAccount and carry one-time charges, they might be directly paid through one or many BusinessPartnerPayments.
