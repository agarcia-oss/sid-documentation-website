Class LoyaltyProgramMember

A LoyaltyProgramMember is defined on each LoyaltyAccount. A LoyaltyProgramMember is a type of PartyRole and is granted with the rights on the LoyaltyAccount such as to execute LoyaltyBurn used to realize a CustomerPayment.
