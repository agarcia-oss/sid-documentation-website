The Figure below shows the associations between SoftwareResource and PartyRole.
One association deals with the licensing of a SoftwareResource by Parties playing a PartyRole. The other association deals with the use of a SoftwareResource.
The figure also shows the attributes that are common to APIs and InstalledSoftware.
Note that there are many attributes and associations inherited by SoftwareResource, such as the association to ResourceUsage from Resource as well as others inherited from LogicalResource (Refer to correspoding section).
