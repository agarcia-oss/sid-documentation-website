Class BusinessInteractionLocation

A Location involved in an interaction. For example, John R.s home address is the location where an ordered Product will be installed.

Attributes

String  locationRole

The locationRole specifies the role of the location (i.e. related Place) for the BusinessInteraction or BusinessInteractionItem. For example, it can be "installation" when related to a PartyOrderItem.
