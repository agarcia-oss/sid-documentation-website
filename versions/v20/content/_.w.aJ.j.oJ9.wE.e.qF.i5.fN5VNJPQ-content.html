An Account specifies basic attributes and relationships that describe an account.
An Account aims to register all Credits and Debits according to criteria. It carries one or many Balances (results of Credits and Debits) and might use one or many Currencies.
Each Account is possessed by one or many PartyRoles such as the Holder or the Payer of a CustomerAccount.
Each Account might be related to other Accounts such as a parent / child relationship.
BusinessPartnerAccount, CustomerAccount and FinancialAccount are types of Account.
A BusinessPartnerAccount and CustomerAccount carries additional information (such as creditLimit) and have specific relationships.
A FinancialAccount is part of the sub-ledger accounting that aggregates the amounts of one or more business partner and / or Customer accounts owned by a given party. Itâ€™s an internal view of the CSP to manage incomes and outcomes.
Each accounting event from a BusinessPartnerAccount or CustomerAccount is logged to a FinancialAccount according to the assignment rules specified by BsuinessPartnerAccountAssignment and CustomerAccountAssignment.
