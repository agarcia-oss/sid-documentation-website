Diagram Figure MS.17 - Competitor Product Correlation Basic Entities
CompetitorProductCorrelations represent a comparison of an enterprise’s ProductOfferings and a ProductOffering’s related entities, such as ProductPromotions, ProductOfferingPrices, and ProductPlacements. Within the SID model, a CompetitorProductCorrelation is represented as information about the association between two ProductOfferings. This is possible since the same model is used to represent an enterprise’s ProductOfferings and those of its Competitors as was previously explained in this addendum. The correlation may be based on Jurisdiction (an area over which authority extends) and/or MarketSegment.
