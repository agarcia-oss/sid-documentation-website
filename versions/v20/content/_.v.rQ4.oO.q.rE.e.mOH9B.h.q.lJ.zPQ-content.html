Diagram Figure P.01a - Party and related Business Entities
A Party may possess one or many PartyAccounts through PartyRoles.
An Account is an arrangement that a Party has with an enterprise (such as a communication service provider) through which the enterprise collects charges to structure bills.
It also collects payments for Products that are supplied by the enterprise to the Party. The charges are invoiced to the Account via bills.
A PartyRole might be evaluated for its worthiness (PartyCreditProfile) and this evaluation might be based on external information (PartyCreditProfileReference). Note: This part is still preliminary and might evolve.
The AccountContact qualifies for an Account a contact point (PartyRole and ContactMedium) such as primary, secondary, and emergency contact.
