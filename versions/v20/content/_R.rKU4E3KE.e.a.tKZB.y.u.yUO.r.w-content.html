Class PartyRevenueShareNotification

A communication of the revenue to be shared to processes and/or a party with whom revenue will be shared. The notification includes the revenue to be shared for all product offerings. For example, to a Party Revenue Sharing Reconciliation process for reconciliation with a received bill/invoice, to Party Payment Management to make a payment to another party, or to a party so that they can reconcile what is to be shared before sending or receiving an bill/invoice.
