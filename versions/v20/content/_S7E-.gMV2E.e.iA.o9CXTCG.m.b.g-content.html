Communication Interaction ABE

The Communication Interaction ABE contains all entities needed for specifing a CommunicationInteraction describing an exchange of information during a communication between two or more human(s) and/or machine(s) during a period of time.
CommunicationInteraction might concern Parties playing a role of Customer, Supplier… or even Resource, therefore the ABE « CommunicationInteraction ABE » is located in « Common Business Entities Domain ».
