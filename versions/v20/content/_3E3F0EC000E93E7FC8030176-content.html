Usage of the Calendar model will be specific to the required usage.
There are two main points in the model at which linking could occur. For instance, we may define a ProjectSchedule and create a Calendar for a Project. Each of the Project Activities can then have a CalendarEntry created showing the start and finish times of the event.
Similarly, a Network Site could have a Calendar defined for it. A battery bank at the site could require regular checking and checking of the water levels could be scheduled every 6 months using a recurring CalendarEvent also inserted into the work queue for the workgroup in charge of maintenance at the site.
The other way that this model can be used is by sub-classing the Calendar and CalendarEntry entities.
To see a more detailed example of the Calendar entities in use, refer to the SID Project model.
