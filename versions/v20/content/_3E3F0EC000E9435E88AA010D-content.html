A RevenueAssuranceViolation is a type of Notification, which in turn is a type of BusinessInteraction. As such it inherits a number of characteristics from BusinessInteraction, such as the date, description, status, and association with Location and association with PartyRole, for example, to whom the violation is assigned. Figure RA.10 shows the key entities that make up the BusinessInteraction ABE. Addendum Common Domain section Business Interaction ABE fully describes the BusinessInteraction ABE.
