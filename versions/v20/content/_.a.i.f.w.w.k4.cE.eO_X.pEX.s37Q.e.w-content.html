Business Partner Domain::Party Bill Collection ABE::Dunning ABE
Class DunningNotification

A DunningNotification is a communication or joint activity from the Telecommunication Operator to the party to ask for a PartyPayment or negotiate a PaymentPlan.
The relation resulting from a DunningCaseRule might be
•    either only from the telecommunication operator to the party (Ex: send a SMS)
•    or in both ways (Ex: a CSR call the party to negotiate a PaymentPlan).
