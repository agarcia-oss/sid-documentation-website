A RunningSoftwareStatistic entity has also been introduced as shown in the Figure below.
RunningSoftwareStatistic represents the characteristics of the running software, such as memory, physical disk, and CPU used. The characteristics are represented by the generalized attribute resourceUse with the type of use represented by the instance of Resource used. The useDateTime attribute records when the Resource is being or has been used.
