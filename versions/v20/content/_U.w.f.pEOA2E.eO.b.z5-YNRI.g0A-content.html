Diagram Figure Me.14 - Metric Applicabilty
A MetricDefMeasure, MetricDefMeasureThresholdRule or even MetricDefMeasureConsequence might applicable only during certain periods.
In this case, the MetricDefMeasureApplicability is used to specify in detail each applicable period.
The MetricDefMeasureApplicability represents the time of day or days during which a MetricDefMeasure, MetricDefMeasureThresholdRule or even MetricDefMeasureConsequence are measurable or not.
The applicableCode attribute Indicates whether an instance represents applicability or un-applicability.
