Diagram Figure Pr.40 - Usage Volume Product Specification
A UsageVolumeProductSpec is a type of AtpmicProductSpecification.
It represents a predefined quantity of product usage (aka bucket), like a 2 hours national calls, often known as an Allowance.
It could be either a quantity or an amount in a currency (i.e. It could represent a fixed number of SMS, MMS, minutes of calls, Quantity of data as well as a specific amount in € …).
A UsageVolumeProductSpec requires one or many NetworkProductSpecs from which Usages will debit the bucket.
A UsageVolumeProductSpec also defines UsageVolumeChargingRules specifying how to decrement the bucket.
A NetworkProductSpec might be monitored or not. The wording monitored means that the user can’t realise a type of usage if he hasn’t any FixedVolumePackageProd for this type of usage (i.e. without an empty balance). In this case, the attribute isUsageMonitoring is TRUE.
