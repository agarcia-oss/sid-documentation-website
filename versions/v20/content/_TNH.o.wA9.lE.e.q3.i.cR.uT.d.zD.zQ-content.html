A ProductOrder represents a request used to procure, update or remove one or many Products in the context of a ProductOffering through all its ProductOrderItems.
Note that ProductSpecifications / Products covers 2 cases: 1) ProductSpecifications / Products bought by a Customer to the Service Provider, 2) ProductSpecifications / Products bought by the Service Provider to Business Partners.
ProductOrder is a sub-class of RootEntity and therefore inherits from all its attributes and relationships.
At least one CommunicationInteraction is the source of a ProductOrder. Then, additional interactions might happen such as the Customer asking why his order isn’t yet delivered or the Service Provider informing the Customer his order is completed.
A ProductOrder might refer to an Agreement specifying all what has been approved by the PartyRoles involved in the ProductOrder.
A ProductOrder and ProductOrderItems might be further described by Attachments.
A ProductOrderItem may need other ProductOrderItems to be completed; this dependency is represented through the ProductOrderItemRelationship.

ProductOrder / ProductOrderItem are sub-classed as
• BusinessPartnerProductOrder used to procure, update or remove Products for the Service Provider
• CustomerProductOrder used to procure, update or remove Products for Customers
