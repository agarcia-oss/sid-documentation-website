One or many PartyRoles might be involved in a CustomerProductOrder / CustomerProductOrderItem such as Distributor, Holder, Buyer or Product User.
Each CustomerProductOrderItem requires an action (AllowedProductAction) on a ProductSpecification or a ProductOffering.
The configuration required is described by the related Product even if it isn’t delivered yet. The prices applicable are related to the Product (ProductPrice) or to ProductOrderItem / PorductOrder when it corresponds to prices not specific to a Product such as a global of 5% on ProductOrder one-time fees.
To deliver the corresponding Product, the Place to deliver and Appointment(s) might be needed. An Appointment is an arrangement to do something or meet someone at a particular time and location.
A CustomerProductOrderItem may also specify the commitment chosen by the Customer (i.e. CommitmentTermOrCondition).
The CustomerProductOrderItems may specify the CustomerAccount to which the Products’ are charged to.
When CustomerProductOrderItems are not charged to a CustomerAccount and carry one-time charges, they might be directly paid through one or many CustomerPayments. The same CustomerPayment may concern several ProductPrices.
CustomerProductOrderItem may reference ServicOrderItems or ResourceOrderItems that participate in the Product required delivery.
Relationships with ServicOrderItem, ResourceOrderItem, CustomerAccount and CustomerPayment are not shared with Business Partner orders.
