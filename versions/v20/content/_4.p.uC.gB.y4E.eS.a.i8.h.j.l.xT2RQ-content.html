Diagram Figure Me.15 - Metric Measure Definition Expression
The following Figure shows how the relationships with MetricDefMeasures might be refined.
For example, a MetricDefMeasureExpression might enable specifying a specific period of applicability or un-applicability (MetricDefMeasureApplicability) for MetricDefMeasureConsequence and / or MetricDefMeasureDefMeasureThresholdRule in the context of a specific MetricDefMeasure.
This capability is useful as MetricDefMeasureConsequence and MetricDefMeasureDefMeasureThresholdRule are reusable by several MetricDefMeasure.
