Customer Product Order ABE

The Customer Product Order ABE handles single customer product orders and the various types thereof, such as regulated and non-regulated orders.

Note:
Some but not all enterprises consider an Order to be a type of an Agreement. From a SID model perspective, an Order can be formalized by an Agreement and the Agreements relationship between the Agreement and the ProductOrder. This philosophy has the advantage of clearly separating two different concepts: the order from the legal formalisms associated with the order. For example, several orders may refer to the same Agreement.
