Common Domain::Party ABE::Party Profile ABE
Class PartyProfileTypeCharacteristic

A characteristic quality or distinctive feature of a PartyProfileType. The characteristic can be take on a discrete value, such as age, can take on a range of values, (for example, investment portfolio value of $50,000 - $100,000), or can be derived from a formula (for example, number of households = number of customer party roles).
