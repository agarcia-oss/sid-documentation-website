Class FulfilledSalesProdSpec

A SalesProductSpecification specifies the role of CSP for a ProductSpecification sold or rented by the Service Provider.
FulfilledSalesProdSpec is a SalesProductSpecification for which the Service Provider take the order and fulfill the product.
