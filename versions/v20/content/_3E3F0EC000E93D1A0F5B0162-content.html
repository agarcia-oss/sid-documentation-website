In addition to product offerings and products, services and resources are also the focus of in business interactions. For example, one service provider may enter into a lease agreement to use a connection belonging to another service provider; the failure of a network device may precipitate the need to send a notification communicating the need to repair the device to a technician, while another notification must be sent to the service subscribes offering an alternative product offering for use by the subscribers.
