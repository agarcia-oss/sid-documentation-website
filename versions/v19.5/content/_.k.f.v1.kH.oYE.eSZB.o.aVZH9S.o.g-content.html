Class SalesProductSpecification

A SalesProductSpecification specifies the role of CSP for a ProductSpecification sold or rented by the Service Provider.
For a SalesProductSpecification the Service Provider might:
•    Either only take the order on behalf of another Party (OrderedOnlySalesProdSpec),
•    Or take the order and fulfill the product (FulfilledSalesProdSpec).
