Diagram Figure Pr.42 - UsageVolume in detail
A ProductUsage might be
• Charged by one or several UsageVolumeBalanceDebit that decrements the corresponding balance
• And might be rated by one or several ProductOfferingPrices that will be charged to an invoice.
An UsageVolumeProduct is an installed UsageVolumeProductSpec.
Each UsageVolumeProduct has at least one balance (UsageVolumeBalance) valid at a given point. A UsageVolumeBalance is initialized when a UsageVolumeChargingRule applies a credit for a new valid period.
Each balance might be decremented by one or several UsageVolumeBalanceDebits and credited by one or several UsageVolumeBalanceCredits.
A UsageVolumeBalanceDebit results from the charging of a ProductUsage.
A ProductUsageCredit results from the application of the rules specified on the UsageVolumeProductSpec through its characteristics (frequency of the credit, quantity of the credit).
