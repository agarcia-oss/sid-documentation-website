Diagram Figure P.07 - Contacting a Party
A PartyRole can be contacted using a contact medium [ACIA], which is an abstraction of the commonly used contact types: email, Postal Address, telephone, fax, etc. We model a PartyRole to having contact mediums rather than a Party. This allows us to cater for role based e-mail addresses and work queues and Contact Details for a POST.
To allow for international usage, we also model the languages and dialects that Parties are able to use. Language Ability tells us which languages a party can converse in.
The Language entity links to Party, not Individual, as it also allows us to represent languages that an Organization supports. For instance, a Service Provider may provide interpreters in certain languages for Customer inquiries.
