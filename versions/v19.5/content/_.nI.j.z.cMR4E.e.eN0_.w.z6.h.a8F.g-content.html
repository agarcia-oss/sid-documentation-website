Product Domain::Product ABE::Usage Volume Product ABE
Class UsageVolumeBalanceCredit

The UsageVolumeBalanceCredit represents an amount in a certain unit that credits the UsageVolumeBalance.
The UsageVolumeBalanceCredit is the result of applying a particular UsageVolumeChargingRule specifying the way to credit the balance (it can be a on-time or recurrent credit) and increments the balance of the UsageVolumeProduct (UsageVolumeBalance).
