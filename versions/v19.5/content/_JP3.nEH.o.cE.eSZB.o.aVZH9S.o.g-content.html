Diagram Figure PPSO.07 - Purchase Product Specification
Several reasons may induce the acquisition of a PurchaseProductSpecification.
It can be:
• Either to realize completely a FulfilledSalesProdSpec,
• Either to realize a CustomerFacingServiceSpec as part of the needs for a FulfilledSalesProdSpec,
• Either to realize a ResourceFacingServiceSpec as part of the needs for a FulfilledSalesProdSpec,
• or to realize a ResourceSpecification whatever it is needed as part of a FulfilledSalesProdSpec, as part of the Service Provider network, etc.
