Diagram Figure R.31 - Characteristic Specs and Pricing
Shown in the following Figure Characteristic Specs and Pricing is one of the promoted “use” association classes and two new price dependency entities. These two entities enable a single or multiple attributes (assuming a simple “and” association between the attributes) to govern a price. In the figure the price is represented as ComponentProdOfferPrice, an existing entity in the Product Offering Price ABE.
More comprehensive requirements, such as and’s and or’s, are supported by adding appropriate attributes to the member entity. An Object diagram follows to show an example.
