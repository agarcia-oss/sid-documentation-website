Diagram Figure PPSO.06 - Fulfilled Sales Product Specification
Several possibilities may exist for the fulfillment of a FulfilledSalesProdSpec:
• Either the Service Provider is able to realize the non-tangible product (realized as a CFServiceSpec),
• either the Service Provider is able to realize the tangible product (realized as a ResourceSpecification),
• Or the ServiceProvider is not able to realize the product and has to buy it to another Party (realized as a PurchaseProductSpecification)
