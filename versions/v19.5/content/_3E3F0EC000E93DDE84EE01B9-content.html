Common Domain::Root Business Entities ABE
Class Entity

This is an abstract class that extends the RootEntity class in order to represent classes of objects that play a business function. Entities can be either managed or unmanaged.
If it is managed, then it is further defined by a ManagedEntity, else it has no relationship with ManagedEntity.
More formally, an Entity represents objects that have a separate and distinct existence. They are not just a collection of attributes or an abstraction of behavior.
