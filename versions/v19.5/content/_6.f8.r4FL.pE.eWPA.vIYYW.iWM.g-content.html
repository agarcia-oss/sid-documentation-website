Diagram Figure PAY.03 - Party and Customer Payment
The following Diagram describes the relationships between CustomerPayment and PartyPayment.
To avoid redundancy between the Customer view and the Party view, the Customer Entities further defines the Party Entities.
But some relationships used between the Party’s Entities are not reused when they are further defined in Customer Entities. In this case, the relationship between Party’s Entities are highlighted in bold pale orange such as the one between PartyPayment and PartyPaymentItem.
PaymentItem is the application of PartyPayment. Each PaymentItem represents an amount applied to PartyBill or AppliedPartyBillingRate.
