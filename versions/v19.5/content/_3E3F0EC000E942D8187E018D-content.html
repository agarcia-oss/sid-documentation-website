Diagram Figure Pr.32 - Product Price
When an enterprise agrees to deliver a product to a customer, one or many ProductOfferingPrice might be identified as applicable for this Product and therefore one or many prices (ProductPrice) are associated with the Product.
The figure below presents the set of entities that are used to hold this information. The ProductPrice represents an amount, usually of money, that represents the actual price paid by a customer for a purchase, a rent or a lease of a Product.
The ProductPrice is associated with the Product entity and the ProductOfferingPrice entity. The structure of ProductPrice subclasses resembles the structure of ProductOfferingPrice subclasses.
The ProductPrice validFor attribute represents the time period during which it is applicable. For example, an alteration might be applicable only 6 months on a recurring charge.
