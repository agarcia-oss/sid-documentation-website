Commitment ABE

The Commitment ABE, derived from the Business Interaction ABE, reflects the mutual agreement between parties about services, products, resources or some other kind of deliverable that has to be provided by the party playing the role of a Commitment fulfiller to the party, playing the role of a Commitment procurer.
