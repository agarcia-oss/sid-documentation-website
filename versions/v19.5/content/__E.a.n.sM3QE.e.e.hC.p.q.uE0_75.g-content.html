Diagram Figure Acc.01 - Account Overview
An Account specifies basic attributes and relationships that describe an account.
An Account aims to register all Credits and Debits according to criteria. It carries one or many Balances (results of Credits and Debits) and might use one or many Currencies.
Each Account is possessed by one or many PartyRoles such as the Holder or the Payer of a CustomerAccount.
Each Account might be related to other Accounts such as a parent / child relationship.
PartyAccount, CustomerAccount and FinancialAccount are types of Account.
A PartyAccount carries additional information (such as creditLimit) and relationships that are common to PartyAccount and CustomerAccount.
A FinancialAccount is part of the sub-ledger accounting that aggregates the amounts of one or more party accounts owned by a given party. Itâ€™s an internal view of the CSP to manage incomes and outcomes.
Each accounting event from a PartyAccount is logged to a FinancialAccount according to the assignment rules specified by PartyAccountAssignment.
