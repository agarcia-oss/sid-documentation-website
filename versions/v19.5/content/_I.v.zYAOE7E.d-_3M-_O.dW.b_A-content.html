Class Commitment

A detailed description of the mutual agreement between parties about services, products, resources or some other kind of deliverable that has to be provided by the party playing the role of a Commitment fulfiller to the party, playing the role of a Commitment procurer. A Commitment may be associated to a KPI to govern the Commitment.
