Diagram Figure Me.18 - Metric Observation
A MetricMeasurementJob might be triggered by the schedule or might be triggered by a request from a PartyRole.
In both cases, a MetricMeasurementObservation tracks each measurement (i.e. execution of a MetricMeasurementJob).
A MetricMeasurementObservation represents the contextual information of a measurement such as the observation period, the MetricMeasurementJob used, the requester of the observation (PartyRole) and the scope of the observation through the related MonitoredMetricObjectCriteria.
The MonitoredMetricObjectCriteria related to MetricMeasurementObservation has to be equal to or a sub-set of the one related to the concerned MetricMeasurementJob.
A MetricMeasurementObservation also specifies the MectricMeasures resulting from this observation.
