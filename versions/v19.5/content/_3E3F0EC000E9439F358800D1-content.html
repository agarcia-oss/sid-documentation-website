Diagram Figure Cat.04 – Product Catalog Entities and Associations
Several additional entities complete the Product Catalog. A DistributionChannel is responsible for providing ProductOfferings in a specific GeographicArea. Each ProductOffering is managed under a ProductOfferingStrategy that supports any number of MarketingCampaigns. MarketingCampaigns are run through SalesChannels, and use ProductCatalogs selected by ProductCatalogSpecification. These entities are shown in the following Figure.
