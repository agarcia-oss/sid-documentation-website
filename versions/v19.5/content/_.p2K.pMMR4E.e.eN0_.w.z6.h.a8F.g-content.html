Product Domain::Product ABE::Usage Volume Product ABE
Class UsageVolumeBalanceDebit

The UsageVolumeBalanceDebit represents an amount in a certain unit that decrements the UsageVolumeBalance.
The UsageVolumeBalanceDebit is the result of applying a particular UsageVolumeChargingRule for a ProductUsage and decrements the balance of the UsageVolumeProduct (UsageVolumeBalance).
