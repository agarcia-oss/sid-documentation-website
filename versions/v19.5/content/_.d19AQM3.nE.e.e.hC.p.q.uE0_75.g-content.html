Class FinancialAccount

A FinancialAccount is part of the sub-ledger accounting that aggregates the amounts of one or more party accounts owned by a given party. It’s an internal view of the CSP to manage incomes and outcomes. It is only an internal accounting which does not need to follow the GeneralLedger accounting regulation.
Each accounting event from a PartyAccount is logged to a FinancialAccount according to the assignment rules specified by PartyAccountAssignment.
