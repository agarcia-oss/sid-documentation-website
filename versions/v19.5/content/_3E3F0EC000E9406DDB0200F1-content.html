Diagram Figure Pr.20 - Product Price Value and Product Offering Price
To support the action associated to a complex rule, another extension is added to the Policy model. The value of the discounted price is not a fixed value, but one associated with a specific Product Offering Price. This can be modelled by first creating a subclass of PolicyValues, called ProdOfferPricePolicyValue, and then creating an association between ProdOfferPricePolicyValue and ProductOfferingPrice, as shown in the figure below.
Note: In all the pricing rule examples, if a condition is not TRUE, then nothing happens.
