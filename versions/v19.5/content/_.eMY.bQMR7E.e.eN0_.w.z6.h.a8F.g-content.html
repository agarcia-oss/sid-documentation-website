Product Domain::Product ABE::Network Product ABE
Class ConsumptionSummary

A ConsumptionSummary give measures about the usage quantity consumed.
It might concern UsageVolumeBalances and / or UsageVolumeProducts and / or NetworkProducts and / or ProductUsers.
A ConsumptionSummary aggregates UsageVolumeBalanceCredits and / or UsageVolumeBalanceDebits and / or AppliedCustomerBillingProductUsageRates.
