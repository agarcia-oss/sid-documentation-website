Common Domain::Metric ABE::Metric Measure ABE
Class MetricMeasure

A numerical value or text determined for a MetricDefMeasure that may be collected for a RootEntity, such as a connection or a dealer.
A MetricMeasure might be part of a group of MetricMeasures realized at a specific moment (MetricMeasureGroup).
For example, a value of .005 ms that represents average packet delay.
