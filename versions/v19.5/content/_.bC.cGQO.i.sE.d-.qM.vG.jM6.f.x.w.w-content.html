Diagram Figure R.34 – Content Charging Product Offer Example
Shown in the following Figure is an example of associating the characteristics with a Product Offering. There are no associated EntitySpecCharValueUses, which implies that all movie titles are available in all formats. The choices can be limited by associating values with the Pay Per View Offering.
By using the generalized characteristic pattern other variations, such as usage-based pricing that is dependent on values associated with usage characteristics or resource characteristics, is also supported.
