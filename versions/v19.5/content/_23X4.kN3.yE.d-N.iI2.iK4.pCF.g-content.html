Class SCRUM_Sprint

SCRUM_Sprint is an element of the ProjectSchedule reflecting a time periode defining a project iteration. The SCRUM_Team commits to the processing of scheduled SCRUM_BacklogItems for a SCRUM_Sprint at the sprint planning meeting. The outcome of every SCRUM_Sprint is a SCRUM_WorkingIncrement presented by the SCRUM_Team to the ProjectCustomer at the sprint review meeting.
