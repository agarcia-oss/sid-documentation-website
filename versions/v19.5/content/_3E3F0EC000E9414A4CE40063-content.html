MarketingCampaigns support a ProductOffering or a specific ProductPromotion that offers incentives to procure a ProductOffering. MarketingCampaigns represent the implementation of ProductOfferingStrategies, which can be correlated to ProductOfferingStrategies of other providers, such as Competitors.
Later in this addendum, the business entities within the Product domain will be reused to include domain details for Competitor and other provider products.
The ChurnAndRetentionStatistics measure the effectiveness of MarketingCampaigns. The figure below shows the associations that MarketingCampaign has with these other business entities.
