Diagram Figure Res.14 - Additional Capacity considerations (OLD RS.05)
The ResourceCapacityDemand represents the amount of ResourceCapacity that is required, planned to be consumed or has been consumed.
The HostingPlatformRequirementSpec requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum…) of capacity required.
A HostingPlatformRequirement is the set of requirements defined by HostingPlatformRequirementSpec which applies to a specific InstalledSoftware. It requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum…) of capacity planned or consumed.
Refer to Common Domain guide book for more explanations about the Capacity model.
