Diagram Figure Me.11 - Metric Measurement Job
The following Figure shows how to define jobs to measure MetricDefMeasures and MetricDefMeasureGroups.

A MetricManagementJob is a management activity that may consist of several steps but is performed as a single logical unit in a Metric context.
It can be a one-time activity or a regular one. In the latter case, a schedule definition is attached to it.

A MetricMeasurementJob always defines a scope of monitored objects using the MonitoredMetricObjectCriteria.
A MetricDefMeasure as well as a MetricDefMeasureGroup may be measured by one or more MetricMeasurementJob.
