Enterprise Domain::Workforce ABE::Work Specification ABE
Class WorkSpecification

WorkSpecification describes the characteristics of a type of (manual) work. The characteristic/characteristic value pattern may be applied for WorkSpecification (and work as an instance). For example, WorkSpecification could be “Assembly of server blade in a shelf”.
