Class PurchaseProductOffering

A PartyRoleProductOffering describes a ProductOffering made available by a PartyRole and the involvement the PartyRole has with the ProductOffering.
A PurchaseProductOffering is a type of PartyRoleProductOffering where the ProductOffering is bought by the Service Provider.
