Diagram Figure P.13 - Party Role and Party Profile Type
A PartyProfile forms the bridge between a PartyRoles or a Parties and a PartyProfileType. The PartyProfile provides a means to keep a record of what PartyProfileTypes were a match at a particular point in time. The PartyRoles that match a profile type help identify the potential market size for a MarketingCampaign, can be the targets of a MarketingCampaign and SalesChannels, and so forth.
