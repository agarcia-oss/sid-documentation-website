Common Domain::Usage ABE
Class ContextUsage

A single usage related entity that is used to represent the three different types of usage, each one specified by the usageState attribute.

Attributes
String  usageState

The state of the usage. Values are "resource usage", "service usage" or "product usage" that indicate possible states in the movement towards product usage, such as from resource to service to product usage.
