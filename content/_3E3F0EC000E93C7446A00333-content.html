Diagram Figure P.06 - Parties Can Be Associated Via Their Roles
We need to be able to show associations between Parties playing roles that are not directly involved in the value fabric.
Examples of these associations include
• Organizational associations in the service provider’s own business (organization unit – employee, organization unit – organization unit)
• Associations in other Organizations participating in the value fabric (organization unit – employee, organization unit – organization unit)
