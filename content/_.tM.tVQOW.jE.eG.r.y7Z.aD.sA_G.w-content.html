Class SecVulnerabilityScoreAlgorithm

A step-by-step procedure for calculating the a security vulnerability score.

Notes: For example, BaseScore = round-to-1-decimal(((0.6 * Impact) + (0.4 * Exploitability) – 1.5) * f(Impact)).
