Service Domain::Service ABE
AssociationClass AdministerServiceDetails

A Service (CustomerFacing or ResourceFacing) that a particular Party, playing a PartyRole, administers. From a business perspective, the Owner has to either appoint or give permission to the Administrator to administer the Service that is owned. This is done using the GrantsServiceAdminRights association class.
