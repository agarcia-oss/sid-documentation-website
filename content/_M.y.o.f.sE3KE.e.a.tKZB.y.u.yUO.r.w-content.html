Class PartyRevenueShare

The amount of revenue shared among parties involved in a revenue sharing agreement. Revenue share is determined using existing artifacts such as usage, invoices, product prices, and applied billing rates. The revenue share may result in a payment to/from a party and/or an bill/invoice to/from a party. It may also be shown as a reduction of or increase to an bill/invoice sent to or received from a party, or a credit/debit to a party's account balance.
