Class PartyRevShareTermOrCondition

The terms and conditions for a party revenue sharing agreement in addition to those specified by an AgreementItem. For example, terms specific to a revenue sharing agreement may include the requirement to aggregate revenue shared for a product offering as well as providing details about each acquisition of an offering.
