Diagram Figure Pr.32a - Product Price Charge hierarchy
The following diagram shows the ProdPriceCharge hierarchy.
• A UsageProdPriceCharge represents a charge applicable to ProductCharges for a Product.
• A OneTimeProdPriceCharge represents a charge to apply for a Product action that has been done such as creation fee.
• A RecurringProdPriceCharge represents a charge that must be applied on a recurring basis for a Product.
