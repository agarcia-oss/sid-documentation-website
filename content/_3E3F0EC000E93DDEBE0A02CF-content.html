Class PhysicalResourceRole

This is representing the different types of roles that various physical resources can take on. Two specialized roles have been defined: holder and adapter. This enables a single object, such as a Card, to have additional functions. For example, a Card may also serve as a motherboard or hosting board for another Card. In this situation, there isn’t a separate EquipmentHolder – rather, the Card acts as a holder in addition to providing its normal functions.
