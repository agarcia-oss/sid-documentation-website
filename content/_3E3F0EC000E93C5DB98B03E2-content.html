Class Product

A ProductOffering obtained by a Customer, or other interested Party playing a PartyRole, appearing as a BusinessInteractionItem, which could take the form of a Agreement. ProductSpecificationCharacteristic(s) in part define the Product. A Product is realized as one or more Service(s) and/or Resource(s).
