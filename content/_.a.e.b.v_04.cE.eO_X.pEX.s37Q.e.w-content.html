Class PartyAllowance

Money (typically) deducted from the selling price of a Product a service provider as a discount or in exchange for something. An example of allowance is 500 free minutes of domestic calls granted to new partys by AllowanceProdPriceAlteration. While a party uses the service the available allowance is gradually reduced. The AvaliablePartyAllowance represents available not yet used allowance.
